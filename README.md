# Tata Car Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
Developed an interactive dashboard using **Power BI** to analyze Tata car sales data and uncover key business trends, fuel preferences, transmission insights, and regional performance patterns. The project transforms raw sales datasets into actionable insights to support data-driven business decisions and improve sales performance.

---

## Project Resources
- Dashboard File: `Tata_Car_Sales_Dashboard.pbix`

---

## Key Features
- Designed an interactive dashboard to visualize sales trends and KPIs
- Built dynamic reports for revenue and car model performance analysis
- Implemented advanced filtering using slicers and date selection
- Enabled real-time KPI tracking for sales amount and vehicle demand
- Created fuel type and transmission analysis dashboards
- Added monthly and state-wise sales performance tracking

---

## Tools & Techniques
- **Tool:** Power BI  
- **Data Processing:** Power Query, Data Cleaning  
- **Visualization:** KPI Cards, Bar Charts, Donut Charts, Line Charts, Tree Maps  
- **Interactivity:** Slicers, Filters, Drill-through Reports  
- **Language:** DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

---

## Dataset Details
- **Sales Information:** Sales Amount, Units Sold
- **Regional Analysis:** State-wise Sales Performance
- **Vehicle Details:** Car Models, Fuel Types, Transmission Types
- **Time Analysis:** Monthly & Yearly Sales Trends
- **Business Insights:** Top Selling Models and Revenue Growth

---

## Key Metrics (KPIs)
- **Total Car Sales:** 190.34K
- **Total Sales Amount:** 123.65bn
- **Most Sales Model:** Curvv
- **Top State by Sales:** Maharashtra
- **Highest Monthly Sales:** 10.88bn (May)
- **Petrol Vehicle Sales:** 76.22K (40.04%)
- **Diesel Vehicle Sales:** 57.16K (30.03%)
- **Electric Vehicle Sales:** 28.65K (15.05%)
- **CNG Vehicle Sales:** 28.31K (14.88%)
- **Manual Transmission Sales:** 79bn
- **Automatic Transmission Sales:** 45bn

---

## Key Insights
- Curvv and Nexon are the highest-selling Tata car models
- Maharashtra, Gujarat, and Tamil Nadu generated the highest sales revenue
- Petrol vehicles contribute the largest share of overall sales
- Manual transmission vehicles dominate total sales performance
- Monthly sales remained stable with peak performance in May
- Electric vehicle sales show growing customer interest

---

## Conclusion
The dashboard provides clear visibility into Tata car sales performance, customer preferences, fuel type trends, and regional sales insights. It helps businesses improve decision-making, track performance, and identify high-performing products and markets effectively.
